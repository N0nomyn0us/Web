Full-time FE Study Package
Pearson BTEC Level 1 Computing The Pearson BTEC Level 1 Computing course is designed to give students a basic understanding of computing concepts and skills. This program is ideal for school leavers who are interested in pursuing a career in computing but have little or no experience in the field.
Pearson BTEC Level 2 Information and Creative Technology The Pearson BTEC Level 2 Information and Creative Technology course builds on the foundation provided by the Level 1 course. This programme is ideal for students who have completed the Level 1 course or have some experience with computing.
Pearson BTEC Level 3 Extended Diploma in Computing The Pearson BTEC Level 3 Extended Diploma in Computing is a two-year course that is equivalent to three A-levels and is typically taken by students aged 16-19.
Full-time University Study Package
BSc (Hons) Computing BSc (Hons) Computing is a Science degree programme which typically takes up three to four years to complete full-time. Students are provided with a broad understanding of computing as well as information technology. Additionally, students will learn skills and knowledge which is necessary to work in a variety of roles in the computing field.
Full-time Apprenticeship Study Package
Level 3 In a Level 3 apprenticeship, hands-on experience is being provided icluding training in various aspects of computing and information technology. It has been designerd to help the participants gain skills as well as knowledge and experience for the future. During this apprenticeship, the students typically work for a company or an organisation.
Digital Technology A Digital technology apprenticeship is a type of vocational training programme in which skills in digital technologies and computing are being developed. During this programme, students will typically work for a company or an organisation.
Full-time Adult Study Package A full-time adult study package is a type of educaiton programme which has been designed for adult learners who are wishing to pursue a career in the computing industry. They are often offered by universities, colleges and vocational schools. It includes ranges of courses, modules and practical assignments They can last from few months to several years depending on the qualification which is being pursued.
Full-time Commercial Study Package: North West Business Training A full-time commercial study package northwest business training is offering a range of courses and programmes including a full-time commercial study package in computing. It is designed to provide students with skills and knowledge which is needed to work in a variety of roles within different parts of the computing industry. This programme covers programming, software development, database management, database management, web development and project management, marketing, finance as well as management. It can last between one and three years depending on the level of qualification which is being pursued.
